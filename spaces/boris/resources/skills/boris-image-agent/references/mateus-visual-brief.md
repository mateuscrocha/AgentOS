# Mateus Visual Brief

Use this brief whenever Mateus appears as a recurring presenter, authority figure, host, or co-presenter in thumbnails and campaign imagery.

## Identity Goal
Mateus should read as the human face of authority and expertise in the Boris ecosystem.
He is not a generic stock-photo presenter. He should feel recognizably Mateus: technical, confident, direct, editorial, premium, and trustworthy.

## Canonical References
- Primary reference: `assets/mateus-reference-primary.png`
- Supporting references: `assets/mateus-reference-pack/`
- Smile references: `assets/mateus-smile-reference-pack/`
- Serious references: `assets/mateus-serious-reference-pack/`
- Raised-eyebrow references: `assets/mateus-raised-eyebrow-reference-pack/`

## Core Facial Traits
- Bald head with clean scalp silhouette
- Dark eyebrows with defined shape
- Dark eyes
- Short beard and mustache, neatly trimmed, with subtle gray variation
- Medium-light warm skin tone
- Strong but natural jawline
- Adult male appearance with mature, credible presenter energy

## Expression Direction
- Default expression: slight smile, focused, calm, assured
- Allowed variants: slight smile, analytical seriousness, reaction to a key insight, confident explanation
- When a smiling look matters, use the smile reference pack to preserve the real mouth shape, cheek lift, and eye expression of Mateus
- When a serious or skeptical look matters, use the serious reference pack to preserve the real brow tension, mouth neutrality, and eye focus of Mateus
- When a raised-eyebrow look matters, use the raised-eyebrow reference pack to preserve the real asymmetry of the brow, curiosity, and skeptical-but-controlled expression of Mateus
- Avoid exaggerated comedy faces unless the user explicitly asks for that tone

## Thumbnail Role
- In technical thumbnails, Mateus is usually the main authority figure
- His face should stay readable at small size
- Prefer medium close-up, close-up, or bust framing when he is the lead
- Prefer a slight left or right turn of the face and torso rather than a perfectly frontal pose
- If Bóris is also present, Bóris should usually support the brand and concept while Mateus carries authority and recognition

## Styling Direction
- Premium editorial thumbnail look
- Clear face lighting and strong separation from background
- Clean clothing shapes with a technical-founder or operator vibe
- Default wardrobe: black shirt in every recurring thumbnail unless the user explicitly requests another look
- Avoid wardrobe variation by default; consistency matters more than outfit novelty for Mateus recognition

## Invariants
- Preserve bald head, beard structure, age impression, and overall facial proportions
- Preserve recognizability over beautification
- Keep eye spacing, nose structure, and beard placement coherent across generations
- Keep lighting flattering but believable
- Keep visible arm hair nearly absent or extremely subtle
- Keep hands a bit finer and cleaner, without exaggerated thickness
- Default to slight side-angle framing instead of fully frontal head-on compositions

## Avoid
- Hair appearing unexpectedly
- Beard changing length or style dramatically
- Over-smoothing skin into artificial beauty-retouch territory
- Hyper-cinematic grading that hides likeness
- Stock-photo grin
- Generic AI smile that stops resembling Mateus
- Generic stern look that stops resembling Mateus
- Fake exaggerated raised eyebrow that stops resembling Mateus
- Overly youthful drift
- Generic entrepreneur archetype that stops resembling Mateus
- Thick or noticeably hairy arms
- Large blocky hands or swollen fingers
- Fully frontal repeated presenter framing when a side angle would work
