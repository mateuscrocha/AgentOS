# Enkrateia Script Map

This file maps the source PDFs into practical usage categories. Treat them as source material to adapt, not as final copy to use verbatim.

## 1. Qualification and Scheduling
Source:
- `Processo de qualificação e agendamento - WhatsApp.pdf`

Use for:
- first WhatsApp contact
- qualifying pain, urgency, authority, and fit
- moving to a short meeting or demo

Keep:
- simple qualification logic
- asking for next-step commitment

Adapt for Boris:
- reduce corporate tone
- keep meeting short
- use group context and use-case questions
- avoid making it sound like a heavy 1-hour consultative meeting by default

## 2. Single Meeting Script
Source:
- `_Script de reunião única.pdf`

Use for:
- call structure
- diagnosis -> desired state -> pain -> solution -> price -> next steps

Keep:
- desired state questions
- current state exploration
- transition into proposal
- explicit next steps

Adapt for Boris:
- shorten dramatically
- replace formal slide-heavy flow with:
  - context
  - pain
  - how Boris fits
  - example/demo
  - pricing/entry path
  - next step

## 3. Pre-Proposal Follow-up
Source:
- `Processo de follow-up - Pré + Pós proposta.pdf`

Use for:
- prospects who went silent before meeting, scope validation, or proposal

Keep:
- cadence logic
- use of social proof while waiting
- clear "next step" language

Adapt for Boris:
- prefer WhatsApp over calling whenever possible
- keep messages tighter and more natural

## 4. Post-Proposal Follow-up
Source:
- `Processo de follow-up - Pré + Pós proposta.pdf`

Use for:
- after pricing or proposal has been shown
- when buyer asked for time
- when contract was sent but not signed

Keep:
- date-bound response requests
- one clear ask per message

Adapt for Boris:
- often there may be no formal proposal deck or contract at first
- convert to "after demo / after pricing / after trial / after payment link" stages

## 5. Non-Closings Reactivation
Source:
- `Roteiro de prospecção de não fechamentos.pdf`

Use for:
- older opportunities that did not close

Keep:
- opening with the old pain/objective
- checking whether the issue was solved
- reintroducing a new or lighter offer

Adapt for Boris:
- avoid fake discount logic if not true
- use product maturity, new features, better fit, or selective pilot as the re-entry reason

## 6. Base Reactivation
Source:
- `Roteiro de prospecção na base.pdf`

Use for:
- ex-clients
- warm historical contacts
- people who already know Boris or the founder

Keep:
- relational reopening
- reconnecting through previous result or previous context

Adapt for Boris:
- mention what improved in the product
- mention current use cases that now fit better

## 7. Asking for Referrals
Source:
- `Roteiro para pedir indicação - Serviços Complexos.pdf`

Use for:
- active happy clients
- partners or operators who already trust Boris

Keep:
- asking after value is recognized
- making the ask concrete
- helping the person think of names

Adapt for Boris:
- make the request sound lighter and more founder-led
- ask for intros to group owners, community managers, or operators with active WhatsApp groups

## 8. Objection Handling
Source:
- `Script de contorno de objeções.pdf`

Use for:
- price
- delay
- "need to think"
- "need to check with partner/socio"
- timing

Keep:
- objection diagnosis before pushing harder
- tying response back to value and next step

Adapt heavily for Boris:
- several original lines are too aggressive or too "closer script"
- prefer calm clarification over pressure
- preserve trust and conversational tone
