# Post Formats

## Technical YouTube

Template:

- Hook with a concrete operational problem
- Define the key question the operator needs answered
- Explain the framework, workflow, or reading logic step by step
- Show one realistic example or before and after interpretation
- Close with practical takeaway and CTA for deeper exploration or demo

Notes:

- Prefer titles framed as problem, method, or diagnosis
- Keep sections explicit so the viewer can follow the reasoning
- Use the product as proof inside the lesson, not as the lesson itself

## Diagnosis

Template:

- Hook with symptom
- Explain hidden cost
- Show why this is common
- CTA asking whether the reader sees this too

## Educational Carousel

Template:

- Slide 1: strong tension
- Slide 2-5: explanation or framework
- Final slide: summary and CTA

## Before and After

Template:

- Describe operation before Bóris
- Show friction, noise, or delay
- Describe operation after Bóris
- CTA to see a real example

## Product Breakdown

Template:

- Hook around a real workflow
- Explain what Bóris does
- Show operational benefit
- CTA for demo or deeper explanation

Adaptation for YouTube:

- expand the workflow into a teachable sequence
- explain decision criteria, not only product output
- include what the operator would miss without this reading layer

## Authority Essay

Template:

- State the thesis
- Reframe a common market assumption
- Back it with practical reasoning
- End with one strong sentence and light CTA
