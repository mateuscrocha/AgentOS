# Editorial Pillars

## 1. Education

Use when teaching the audience how to think about group operations, communities, activity patterns, summaries, and decision-making.

Good angles:

- what to monitor
- how to identify recurring themes
- how to read engagement
- what healthy operations look like

## 2. Pain and Diagnosis

Use when the goal is recognition of problems and urgency.

Good angles:

- invisible costs of improvisation
- teams trapped in manual reading
- active groups with low strategic value
- recurring errors hidden by volume

## 3. Product in Practice

Use when showing Bóris in realistic scenarios.

Good angles:

- summaries
- detection of repeated doubts
- pattern reading
- before and after operational clarity

## 4. Authority and Vision

Use when building category language and market leadership.

Good angles:

- the future of group operations
- intelligence layers over messaging environments
- why context matters more than raw volume

## 5. Conversion

Use when the content should drive a direct next step.

Good angles:

- demo invitations
- fit qualification
- scenario-led calls to action
- diagnostics and consultation
